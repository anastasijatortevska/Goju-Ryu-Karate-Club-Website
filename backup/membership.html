Membership

To join the club, you must have membership to the Dodge Fitness Center (DFC), available to students, faculty, alumni and other affiliates of Columbia University. Most Columbia students have automatic access to the DFC. If you are unsure whether you are eligible for DFC membership, more information can be found here.

You can sign up for our mailing list to receive recent news and updates.

For Newcomers

  • Come try a few classes! You are granted 3-4 free classes to get a feel for our dojo.
  • If you do not own a gi (karate uniform), come dressed in comfortable workout clothes.

For Club Members

  • Membership dues are $50 per semester for undergraduate members, and $75 per semester for other members. If you enter a tournament, entry fees are covered by the club.
  • If you do not own a gi (karate uniform), please speak to one of the officers.
